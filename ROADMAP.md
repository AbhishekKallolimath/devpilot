# 🚀 DevPilot Roadmap

## Vision

DevPilot is a developer productivity and DevOps assistant designed to simplify everyday development tasks through a single command-line interface.

Our goal is to automate repetitive tasks, improve developer workflows, and provide quick insights into the development environment.
